Towers of Hanoi

about

Towers of Hanoi is a puzzle game, traditionally played wooden disks. This is a recreation of the game in javascript.

board

board for towers of hanoi

controls

Use the numbers 1, 2, and 3 to select the source and destination of the move.
Use the up and down arrows to configure the number of disks in the game.
Press the r key to reset the disks and the timer.
Press the s key to let the computer solve the puzzle.
